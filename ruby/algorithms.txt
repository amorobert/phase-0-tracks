#Elevator Algorithm
-Choose what number floor you want to visit(An integer value)
-For each number button
  -Check if the buttons face value is equal to your desired floor number
    -If the button is equal to your floor
      -Press it!
    -If the button is not equal to your floor
      -Move on to the next floor
    -If none of the buttons match your floor
      -Find a taller building buddy!
